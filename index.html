About Us

Welcome to Thikki Baa, your go-to mobile bar for vibrant, Indian-inspired mocktails! We’re passionate about crafting refreshing, beautifully balanced drinks that celebrate the rich flavors of India — from aromatic spices to tropical fruits.

Whether it’s a wedding, birthday, corporate event, or any celebration, our mobile bar brings the magic to your doorstep. Every mocktail is thoughtfully designed to be as visually stunning as it is delicious, so your guests can sip, savor, and share memorable moments.

Our Baa's Legacy

The beverages are named after the Baa's we loved dearly and lost. The flavours of their food still linger, and this business is an attempt to capture their love and legacy. Kunwar Baa, Nandi Baa, Paali Baa, Jamna Baa, and Amba Baa are some of the blends you will experience!

It's not just about Thikki Baa's innovation, but a passion for tradition and family that brings this business to life.

Events & Mobile Bar

Bespoke Bar Experience

Elevate your next event with Thikki Baa, the ultimate mobile bar service delivering a truly unforgettable experience. Forget standard bar setups—we bring a burst of color and unique charm with our distinctive Indian-inspired mobile bar. Designed to be a focal point, the Thikki Baa setup adds a vibrant, festive atmosphere to any occasion, be it a lavish wedding, a memorable birthday celebration, or a high-energy corporate event. We deliver a complete, curated bar environment that transforms your venue and perfectly complements the celebratory mood.

Consciously Crafted

At Thikki Baa, our commitment to an exceptional experience extends to the planet. We are proudly eco-friendly, consciously implementing sustainable practices and using compostable cups and napkins to minimize our environmental footprint at every event. We believe in purity and quality, which is why all the ingredients used in our unique drinks—from our vibrant garnishes to our fresh juices—are sourced as organic produce. Furthermore, our entire curated menu is intentionally designed to be 100% vegan-friendly, ensuring that every guest can enjoy our colorful creations without compromise.

Thikki Baa: Terms & Conditions

1. Booking and Payment

1.1. Deposit: A non-refundable deposit of 25% of the total estimated service fee is required to secure the Event date. The booking is not confirmed until the deposit is received.

1.2. Final Payment: The remaining balance is due 15 days prior to the Event date. Failure to pay on time may result in cancellation with no refund of the deposit.

1.3. Additional Costs: Any agreed-upon additions must be paid in full prior to the Event.

1.4. Pricing: Prices are subject to change until the deposit is paid and booking confirmed.

2. Cancellations and Changes

2.1. Cancellation by Client:

  • More than 60 days before: Deposit refunded.
  • 30 days before: 20% of total service fee retained.
  • Less than 15 days before: 100% of total service fee due.

2.2. Postponement: We will try to transfer the booking to a new date. If we cannot accommodate the new date, the cancellation policy applies.

2.3. Service Changes: Significant changes (e.g., guest count) must be requested in writing at least 14 days prior.

3. Service Scope and Safety

3.1. Non-Alcoholic Service: We provide exclusively non-alcoholic beverages. We will not purchase, possess, or serve alcohol.

3.2. Prohibited Items: We reserve the right to cease service if the Client or guests attempt to mix alcohol using our equipment. No refund will be provided.

3.3. Allergies: Please inform us of severe allergies 14 days prior. Guests with allergies consume products at their own risk.

4. Event Requirements

4.1. Access: We require clear access and setup 60 minutes prior to service start.

4.2. Utilities: Client must provide access to power and a clean water source.

4.3. Setup: Equipment must be set up on a stable, level surface clear of trip hazards.

5. Staffing and Overtime

5.1. Duration: Minimum service duration is five hours.

5.2. Overtime: Extensions are subject to agreement and billed at R80.00 per staff member per 30 minutes, due immediately after the Event.

6. Weather

6.1. Outdoors: Client must provide adequate shelter (tent/canopy) to protect bar/staff from rain, sun, or wind.

6.2. Severe Weather: We reserve the right to cease service if weather poses a safety risk. No refunds for inadequate shelter.

7. Liability

7.1. Indemnity: Client agrees to indemnify the Company against claims arising from the Event, except in cases of gross negligence by the Company.

7.2. Damage: Client is financially responsible for damage to equipment caused by guests or other vendors.
